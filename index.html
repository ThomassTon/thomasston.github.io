Haolei Tong

Hey! This is Haolei Tong. I am currently working at Neura Robotics GmbH, focusing on imitation learning, vision-language-action (VLA) models, and bimanual manipulation. I hold a master's degree in Autonomous Systems and Robotics from TU Darmstadt. I completed my master's thesis at the PEARL Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Georgia Chalvatzaki. Additionally, I am a Formula 1 fan, and in my free time, I enjoy cycling.
